2% revenue growth does not suggest plain
sailing for telcos

The waters look calm in the telecom space. However,

companies need to work hard to maintain their top-line

growth, especially in markets with many operators. We see

competition coming from new satellite services and new ﬁbre

operators, often competing on price. Also, in the business

segment, we continue to see the impact of over-the-top

services, like Teams and WhatsApp. Telecom companies have

to step up their game to bring competitive packages to small

and medium-sized businesses. KPN oﬀers a good example of a

company that has renewed its SME oﬀers.
